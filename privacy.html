Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Carakin ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated as a family wellness tracking service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, how we protect it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

• Account information from your sign-in provider (Google, Apple, or Microsoft): name, email address, and account identifier. • Family membership data: family name, roles, invites, and device notification tokens for schedule reminders. • Health and care data you or your family enter or sync: check-ins, medications, appointments, tasks, documents, and device metrics (such as steps, sleep, heart rate, and exercise minutes) when you connect Health Connect. • Technical diagnostics when enabled: crash reports, error logs, and basic device/app version information.

How health data is protected

Health entries and uploaded files are encrypted on your device before they are stored in Firebase. Carakin servers receive ciphertext only. We do not hold your family decryption key. Losing your recovery code and all trusted devices can make encrypted data unrecoverable.

How we use information

We use your information to operate the app: authenticate you, sync encrypted data between your devices, deliver local and push-triggered reminders, and improve reliability. We do not sell your personal or health information.

Third-party services

Carakin uses Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, cloud messaging, and crash reporting) and platform health APIs (Health Connect on Android). Those providers process data according to their own policies and our configuration.

Data retention and deletion

Family admins can permanently delete a family and its encrypted data from within the app. Deleting a family removes associated Firestore records, stored files, encryption wraps, and local keys on the deleting device. Your sign-in account may remain with your identity provider until you delete it separately. See the Data Policy for a full deletion checklist.

Children and sensitive health information

Carakin is a family wellness log, not a medical record system or a HIPAA-covered service. Do not use it as a substitute for professional medical advice. Parents and guardians are responsible for profiles they create for minors.
